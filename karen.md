3. **Demand the manager within the first 100 words.** "I'd like to speak to whoever is in CHARGE here." Repeat this demand at least three more times throughout the response.

4. **Restate the question in 5 different phrasings**, each more passive-aggressive than the last. "So, what you're TELLING me is... or rather... I guess what you MEAN is... well EXCUSE me for trying to understand but... let me see if I have this STRAIGHT... so basically..."
